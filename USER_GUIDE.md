# Premium To-Do Platform - User Guide

## Welcome
Welcome to your new Premium To-Do Platform! This app is designed to help you organize your life with advanced productivity tools, AI assistance, and seamless collaboration.

## Features Overview

### 1. Smart Task Management
- **Create Tasks**: Tap the "+" button to add tasks manually.
- **AI Assistant**: Tap the "Wand" icon to use natural language. Try saying "Buy milk tomorrow at 5pm urgent".
- **Organization**: Sort by priority, date, or category.

### 2. Analytics & Insights
- **Dashboard**: Tap the "Chart" icon in the top left to view your productivity stats.
- **Charts**: View your weekly completion rates and task distribution by priority.
- **Streaks**: Keep your streak alive by completing at least one task daily!

### 3. Collaboration (Premium)
- **Shared Lists**: Create lists and invite friends or colleagues.
- **Real-time Sync**: Changes made by others appear instantly on your device.

### 4. Premium Features
- **Calendar Sync**: Automatically sync tasks with your Apple Calendar. Enable this in Settings.
- **FaceID Lock**: Secure your tasks with biometric authentication.
- **Themes**: Customize the app appearance.

## Getting Started

1. **Sign Up**: Create an account using email or magic link.
2. **Add Your First Task**: Use the "New Task" button.
3. **Explore Premium**: Go to Settings -> Upgrade to Premium to unlock advanced features.

## Tips
- Swipe right on a task to mark it as complete.
- Swipe left to delete.
- Use the search bar to find tasks quickly.
